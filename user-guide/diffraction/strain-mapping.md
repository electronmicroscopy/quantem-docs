# Strain mapping

Strain and rotation mapping from 4D-STEM data, implemented in `quantem.diffraction.strain` and `quantem.diffraction.model_fitting`. Two approaches are available: lattice fitting to detected Bragg disks, and direct model fitting to the diffraction patterns.
