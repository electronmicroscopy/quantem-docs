# Imaging

`quantem.imaging` covers analysis of real space images: S/TEM images of atomic lattices and image time series.

Current capabilities:

- [Drift correction](./drift-correction.md): measure and correct scan drift and distortion in image series.
- [Lattice analysis](./lattice-analysis.md): fit atomic lattices, and measure lattice distortions.

Runnable examples live in the [imaging tutorials](https://github.com/electronmicroscopy/quantem-tutorials/tree/main/tutorials/imaging).
