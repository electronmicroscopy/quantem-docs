# Citing quantEM

Please cite the paper describing the method you used. If the method has no paper yet, cite the software itself. Citing both is welcome when a method paper covers only part of your workflow.

## Method papers

Cite these when your work uses the corresponding method.

**Iterative ptychography with deep generative priors**
A. R. C. McCray, S. M. Ribet, G. Varnavides, C. Ophus, *Deep generative priors for robust and efficient electron ptychography*, 2025. [arXiv:2511.07795](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.07795)

**Direct ptychography**
G. Varnavides, J. M. Bekkevold, S. M. Ribet, M. C. Scott, L. Jones, C. Ophus, *Relaxing direct ptychography sampling requirements via parallax imaging insights*, Microscopy and Microanalysis **32**, 2 (2026). [doi:10.1093/mam/ozaf139](https://doi.org/10.1093/mam/ozaf139)

**Tomography with implicit neural representations**
C. Lim, C. Casert, A. R. C. McCray, S. Lee, A. Barnum, J. Dionne, C. Ophus, *Missing wedge inpainting and joint alignment in electron tomography through implicit neural representations*, 2025. [arXiv:2512.08113](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.08113)

**Multi-angle precession electron diffraction (MAPED)**
S. M. Ribet, R. Dhall, C. Ophus, K. C. Bustillo, *Multi-angle precession electron diffraction (MAPED): a versatile approach to 4D-STEM precession*, Microscopy and Microanalysis **31**, 6 (2025). [doi:10.1093/mam/ozaf103](https://doi.org/10.1093/mam/ozaf103)

## Software

Cite the software when no method paper applies, or alongside a method paper. The concept DOI below always resolves to the latest release; use the release-specific DOI from [Zenodo](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18642593) if you need to pin an exact version.

> quantEM Authors (2026). *quantEM: quantitative electron microscopy analysis toolkit*. [doi:10.5281/zenodo.18642593](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18642593)

```bibtex
@software{quantem,
  author  = {{quantEM Authors}},
  title   = {quantEM: quantitative electron
             microscopy analysis toolkit},
  year    = {2026},
  doi     = {10.5281/zenodo.18642593},
  url     = {https://github.com/electronmicroscopy/quantem},
  license = {MIT},
}
```

The authors, in alphabetical order, are Karen Ehrhardt, Matthew Feickert, Carter Francis, Sangjoon Bob Lee, Cedric Lim, Darshan Mali, Nicholas Marchese, Arthur R. C. McCray, Will Millsaps, Colin Ophus, Stephanie M. Ribet, and Georgios Varnavides.

The authoritative, per-release metadata lives in [CITATION.cff](https://github.com/electronmicroscopy/quantem/blob/main/CITATION.cff) in the repository. GitHub renders it under "Cite this repository", and most reference managers import it directly.
